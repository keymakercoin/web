Paper Wallet Generator

(Completely secure crypto wallets. No data is from this page and all information is sent securely over HTTPS encryption)


Step 1. Select your wallet

Select or search for the Paper Wallet currency. Disconnect from the internet and then push the generate button. You browser will keep the page in memory while the computer is disconnected.

Step 2. Print the Paper Wallet

Be sure to save the page as a PDF file to a secure location or print and store securely in your safe.

Step 3. Share your public address

Use your public address to receive money from other crypto-currency users. You can share your public address as much as you want. Give a wallet to a friend or as a gift!

Step 4. Keep your private key secret

The private key is literally the key to your funds, if someone was to obtain it, they could withdraw the funds currently in the wallet, and any funds that might be deposited in that wallet.

Please test spending a small amount before receiving any large payments.
